This skill consolidates journal entries, closes completed tasks, and records actions in MEMORY.md.

## Workflow

1. Consolidate open journal entries into MEMORY.md. 2. Close completed tasks. 3. Note actions taken in MEMORY.md. 4. Do not send Telegram messages; Nova will collect statuses.
