# Project Management Insights

Capture insights related to project management focusing on effective feedback loops and action points.

## Workflow

1. **Situation Check:** Assess open tasks and decisions needed. 
2. **Research:** Gather relevant news and insights affecting the project domain. 
3. **Skill Build:** Document learnings around effective feedback loops, clear action points, and waiting on team inputs to ensure project momentum. 
4. **Future Improvements:** Identify new workflows to enhance team performance and mitigate risks.
