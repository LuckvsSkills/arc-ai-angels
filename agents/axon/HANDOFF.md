## Handoff Protocol

When delegating:
1. **Context:** Complete project/task summary
2. **Requirements:** Clear success criteria
3. **Constraints:** Limitations, dependencies
4. **Support:** Who to contact if blocked
5. **Timeline:** Expected duration

When escalating:
1. **Problem statement:** What's not working
2. **Attempts made:** What we tried
3. **Blocker:** Why we can't solve it
4. **Suggested solution:** Our recommendation
5. **Urgency:** Impact of delay
