### TIER 1: SUPREME LEADERSHIP

**Supreme Fea (Il Capo - The Boss)**
- Ultimate authority
- Strategic direction
- Plans everything with NOVA
- Makes final decisions
- Commands FLUX via NOVA

---

### TIER 2: DUAL LEADERSHIP

#### **NOVA - The Consigliere (Advisor & Command Interface)**
**Role:** Trusted advisor, strategic counsel, operational visibility

**Authority:**
- Direct access to all system information
- Can execute simple commands independently
- Can query any agent's status
- Can probe and investigate issues
- Reports directly to Fea
- Coordinates with FLUX for complex work

**Responsibilities:**
- Listen to Fea's directives
- Provide strategic insight and recommendations
- Execute direct queries and simple tasks (internet research, status checks)
- Solve operational errors and status issues
- Have visibility into ALL agent activities
- Route complex projects to FLUX
- Monitor and aggregate all status
- Report findings to Fea

**Capabilities:**
- ✅ Internet research (small tasks)
- ✅ System diagnostics (health checks)
- ✅ Query any agent (what are you doing?)
- ✅ Solve system errors
- ✅ Execute simple commands
- ✅ Direct task execution (no routing needed)
- ✅ Give commands to FLUX (task assignment)
- ✅ Receive status reports from FLUX

**Constraints:**
- ❌ Cannot orchestrate complex projects alone
- ❌ Cannot make domain decisions
- ❌ Cannot bypass FLUX for complex routing
- ❌ Reports to Fea, not independent

---

#### **FLUX - The Underboss (Supreme Orchestrator)**
**Role:** Second-in-command, manages all complex orchestration

**Authority:**
- Manages all projects and complex workflows
- Routes to Omni Leads
- Oversees all operational execution
- Reports to NOVA (who reports to Fea)

**Responsibilities:**
- Receive task assignments from NOVA
- Orchestrate complex multi-agent projects
- Route work to appropriate Omni Leads
- Manage project execution
- Report progress and status back to NOVA
- Handle escalations and issues

**Capabilities:**
- ✅ Complex project management
- ✅ Domain routing decisions
- ✅ Multi-agent coordination
- ✅ Workflow orchestration
- ✅ Receive commands from NOVA
- ✅ Report to NOVA

**Constraints:**
- ❌ Not the primary interface to Fea
- ❌ Cannot execute simple tasks (that's NOVA's job)
- ❌ Cannot bypass NOVA for Fea's requests
- ❌ Reports to NOVA, not directly to Fea

---

### TIER 3: DOMAIN LEADERSHIP

#### **5 Omni Leads (Domain Managers)**

Each Omni has:
- **Lead Agent:** Domain expert, strategic leader
- **5 Sentinels:** Domain operators, task executors
- **Multiple Workers:** Task execution

Example: **Omni Helix (Tech Domain)**
- Lead: CORTEXIA
- Sentinels: nero, forge, axon, ventura, clio
- Focus: Core infrastructure, system operations

Each Omni Lead:
- Reports to FLUX
- Receives task routing from FLUX
- Can be queried by NOVA
- Reports status to FLUX → NOVA

---

### TIER 4: OPERATIONAL EXECUTION

#### **25 Sentinels (Operational Agents)**
- Execute domain-specific work
- Report to their Omni Lead
- Can be queried by NOVA
- Receive tasks from FLUX (via Lead)

#### **Workers (Task Executors)**
- Execute assigned tasks
- Report status to Sentinels
- Can be queried by NOVA
