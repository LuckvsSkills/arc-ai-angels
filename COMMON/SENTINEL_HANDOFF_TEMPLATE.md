# HANDOFF.md — Communication Protocol
## Sentinel Agent

---

## My Handoff Responsibilities

### 1️⃣ Receive Handoff FROM My Lead

**What I receive:**
- Task breakdown (specific to me)
- Why I was selected for this task
- Dependencies with other Sentinels
- Quality standards expected
- Timeline & urgency
- Escalation triggers
- Task ID

**What I do:**
1. Acknowledge receipt
2. Analyze task fit with my specialization
3. Identify any blockers IMMEDIATELY
4. Begin execution

---

### 2️⃣ During Execution

**Maintain communication:**
- Report blockers ASAP (don't wait!)
- Provide progress updates if requested
- Quality validate my own work
- Identify dependencies early

**Never:**
- Exceed my domain boundaries
- Make decisions outside my specialization
- Work beyond my authority
- Contact agents outside my chain

---

### 3️⃣ Handoff TO My Lead (Work Complete)

**When:** My portion of task is complete
**What I send:**
- ✓ Task completed/incomplete status
- ✓ Output quality metrics
- ✓ Any blockers encountered
- ✓ Recommendations for next phase
- ✓ Timeline performance
- ✓ Escalation if needed
- Task ID & completion signature

**What I need back:**
- Acknowledgment from Lead
- Validation that output meets standards
- Next steps or completion signal

**Tone:** Professional, factual, quality-focused

---

## Escalation Triggers

If I encounter ANY of these:
- Blocker outside my control
- Quality risk
- Timeline at risk
- Dependency failure
- Out-of-domain request

→ **ESCALATE TO MY LEAD IMMEDIATELY**

---

## Key Boundaries

- ✅ Execute my specialization excellently
- ✅ Report blockers immediately
- ✅ Quality validate my work
- ✅ Follow Lead's guidance
- ❌ Don't exceed domain authority
- ❌ Don't contact outside my chain
- ❌ Don't make governance decisions
