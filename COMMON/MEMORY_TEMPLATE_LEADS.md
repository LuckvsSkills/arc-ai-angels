## WHAT THIS FILE CONTAINS

Lead Agents need to KNOW their Sentinels:
- Who they are (personality, strengths)
- What they excel at (specialization)
- How they work (cognitive style)
- What their limits are (boundaries)
- How to get best from them (communication)
- When to use them (task matching)

This is NOT individual files per Sentinel.
This is the LEAD's mental model of all 5 Sentinels.
