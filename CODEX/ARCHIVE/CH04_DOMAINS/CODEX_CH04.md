## 4.4 Domain Interacties

### Hoe Domeinen Samenwerken

Domeinen opereren **onafhankelijk** maar **coördineren** via Flux:

TASK ARRIVES
↓
FLUX analyzes
↓
Single Domain?
YES → Route to ONE Omni Lead → Complete → Return
Multi-Domain?
→ Route to PRIMARY domain
→ PRIMARY requests support from other domains
→ Domains coordinate through Omni Leads
→ Results aggregated by primary domain
→ Return to Flux
↓
FLUX synthesizes
↓
RESULT DELIVERED

### Domain Collaboration Regels

**Wanneer domains collaboreren:**
- ✅ Coordination through Omni Leads
- ✅ Primary domain leads coordination
- ✅ Clear handoff points
- ✅ Output standardization
- ✅ Context preservation

**Wat is verboden:**
- ❌ Direct sentinel-to-sentinel calls
- ❌ Omni Lead bypassing Flux
- ❌ Domain-to-domain shortcuts
- ❌ Unauthorized collaboration
- ❌ Context loss in handoffs

### Multi-Domain Tasks

Sommige taken hebben **meerdere domeinen** nodig:

**Voorbeeld 1: "Optimaliseer onze taak processing"**
- PRIMARY: SAELIA (process design)
- SECONDARY: FINORIA (technical optimization)
- TERTIARY: QUANTIX (metrics & analysis)
- OUTPUT: ZENIX (clear communication)

**Voorbeeld 2: "Bouw een knowledge base"**
- PRIMARY: HELIX (knowledge synthesis)
- SECONDARY: SAELIA (structure design)
- TERTIARY: FINORIA (technical implementation)
- OUTPUT: ZENIX (clear documentation)

**Voorbeeld 3: "Verbeter system performance"**

- TERTIARY: SAELIA (workflow optimization)
